About NoteStream

NoteStream is a collaborative lecture annotation platform for the web. No more scattered notebooks, no more printed slide decks.

Teachers upload slides to NoteStream, where students watch edits happen in real-time. Share your notes with friends, markup slides together, and track text and code markup alongside slides.

NoteStream makes hand-raising old school by also incorporating scoreboard-style ranked questions. Students can post questions alongside slides, tag those questions, and vote up questions by peers they feel are most relevant.
